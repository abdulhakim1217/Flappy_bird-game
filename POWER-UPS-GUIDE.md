## üí° Pro Tips

### Tip #1: Stacking
You can have **multiple power-ups active** at once!
- Shield + Slow Motion = Easy mode
- Magnet + Double Points = Score explosion
- All 4 active = God mode!

### Tip #2: Timing
- Activate Double Points before pipe-heavy sections
- Use Shield before difficult gaps
- Save Slow Motion for high-speed levels

### Tip #3: Priority
If you can only grab one:
1. **Double Points** (if you're good at the game)
2. **Shield** (if you're struggling)
3. **Magnet** (if coins are nearby)
4. **Slow Motion** (if speed is too high)

### Tip #4: Risk vs Reward
- Don't risk a life for a power-up
- Power-ups respawn frequently
- Lives are more valuable than power-ups
